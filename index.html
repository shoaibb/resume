I have more than 4 years of experience in data management and handling of research data at the Schools of Medical Sciences and Health Sciences. At a strategic level, I participate in meetings and collaborates with different stakeholders in order to implement and maintain technically efficient and legally compliant data management infrastructure. At the operational level, I provide support to researchers in properly following data management routines in their everyday work with regards to data handling in a GDPR compliant manner.
